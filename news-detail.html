Annual Sports Day 2026

Al Nahda International Schools celebrated its Annual Sports Day with remarkable enthusiasm and participation from all grade levels. The event showcased the athletic prowess, competitive spirit, and sportsmanship of our diverse student body in a day filled with excitement and achievement.

Event Highlights

The sports day featured a comprehensive program of activities designed to engage students across various athletic disciplines. From track and field events to relay races, obstacle courses, and team sports competitions, every student had the opportunity to participate and contribute to their house teams.

  • 100m Sprint - Lightning-fast performances showcased incredible speed
  • Long Jump & High Jump - Students demonstrated impressive athletic abilities
  • Relay Races - Exciting inter-class competitions fueled by team spirit
  • Obstacle Course - Challenging activities tested agility and determination
  • Team Sports - Football, basketball, and volleyball tournaments throughout the day

Celebrating Excellence

Throughout the day, students displayed not only their physical capabilities but also exemplary values of teamwork, perseverance, and sportsmanship. Teachers and parents witnessed incredible moments of support, encouragement, and celebration as classmates cheered for their peers.

The closing ceremony recognized outstanding performances and awarded trophies to winning teams. However, the true victory was the participation of every student and the bonds strengthened through friendly competition and shared experiences.

Looking Forward

The Annual Sports Day remains a cornerstone of Al Nahda's commitment to holistic student development. We are proud of our students' athletic achievements and their demonstration of our core values. We look forward to an even more dynamic and inclusive sports program in the coming year.

Thank you to all the teachers, staff, parents, and volunteers who made this event possible. Your dedication ensures that our students have opportunities to grow, excel, and build lasting memories.
